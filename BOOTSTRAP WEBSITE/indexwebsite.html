When we Heal the Earth, We Heal ourselves!

Our project title is Naturvård which is a Swedish word for nature conservation . As it is a Swedish word its new for everyone which makes it unique from other’s title. Our title is self describing as it means protection of nature and we are working on the wellness of nature and helping out people to reach awareness about plants and its health simultaneously we are working on recycling the tetra packs also. If we look towards the future of the project we are planning to spend all our profit on spending further on our environment and looking for more renewable sources of energy which are less known in today’s world and further improving our services.The future depends on what we do today. Being able to connect the supplier and collector without a middleman is a great outcome as it helps in cost reduction. Our project also helps farmers know the effective and efficient methods to use their resources during the 3 agricultural seasons for the best quality and profitable yield. Any step taken towards the conservation of resources and using them efficiently will be a success for our project. The outcome of this project is to be able to spread awareness about recycling possible resources and encouraging the use of lesser-known renewable sources of energy. All these small steps add to a greater cause and will help in the distant future
